About the Property

Where Architecture
Meets the Pacific

Set behind a canopy of centuries-old oleander trees along one of Montecito's most coveted private drives, this newly completed estate redefines California living. Conceived by a collaboration between internationally acclaimed architects and the region's most celebrated landscape designers, every surface, sightline, and material has been considered with an uncompromising commitment to craft.

The main residence unfolds across a single story of white oak, hand-finished plaster, and floor-to-ceiling glass that frames uninterrupted views of the Channel Islands. A central courtyard — open to the sky — anchors the plan, drawing natural light deep into the living quarters while preserving an atmosphere of serene privacy. The primary suite occupies its own wing, with a private garden, a freestanding soaking tub aligned to the morning sun, and a dressing room that rivals the finest Parisian ateliers.

Outside, the grounds descend through terraced gardens to an infinity-edge pool that appears to merge with the Pacific horizon. A detached guest pavilion, professional outdoor kitchen, and championship-grade tennis court complete a property that offers not merely a home, but a way of life reserved for the most discerning few.
