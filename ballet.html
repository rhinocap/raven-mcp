Our Story

Started by a mom who couldn't find what she wanted

When our daughter started ballet at four, we wanted gear that was as beautiful as the art form itself — not scratchy polyester in garish colors. So we started making it ourselves.

Every piece is designed with care: soft fabrics that let little bodies move freely, colors inspired by real ballet costumes, and details that make girls feel like they're already on stage.
