# Raven MCP — Brand & Mythology

## Domain
ravenmcp.ai

## Name Origin
Named after the raven's role across three mythological traditions — Norse, Celtic, and Native American — as the creature that bridges worlds, carries knowledge, and brings what was hidden into the light. "Rune" was also considered (Odin's runes = knowledge through sacrifice) but Raven won.

## The Mythology

### Norse — Huginn & Muninn (Thought & Memory)
Odin's two ravens fly across all nine realms at dawn, returning to whisper everything they've witnessed. Huginn = thought/knowledge, Muninn = memory/mind. In Grímnismál (Poetic Edda), Odin fears losing Muninn more than Huginn — memory is the deeper faculty. Odin's kenning: Hrafnaguð ("Raven-God"). The ravens are his externalized consciousness. Vikings carried the hrafnsmerki (raven banner) into battle. Runes and ravens share the same root: wisdom gained through sacrifice and relentless seeking.

### Celtic — The Morrígan & Brân the Blessed
The Morrígan ("Phantom Queen") — Irish triple goddess of war, fate, and sovereignty — takes raven form. Badb Catha ("Battle Crow") decides the slain. In Welsh myth, Brân fab Llŷr (brân = "raven") is a giant-king whose severed prophetic head was buried at the Tower of London to protect Britain. Ravens are sovereignty birds. Druids practiced corvid augury — reading raven flight for prophecy.

### Native American — Raven the Transformer
Tlingit: Yéil (Raven) brought light to the world by stealing sun, moon, and stars from an old chief's bentwood boxes. Shapeshifted into a hemlock needle, was reborn as the chief's grandson, released the celestial bodies, flew through the smokehole — soot turning white feathers black forever. Haida: Nang Kilslas ("He Whose Voice Is Obeyed"). Bill Reid's sculpture: Raven discovering first humans in a clamshell. Raven brings light (knowledge), water (life), fire (technology) through cunning. He is transformation itself.

### The Thread
All three: the raven bridges worlds, carries knowledge, and brings what was hidden into the light.
- Norse = thought + memory flying across realms
- Celtic = prophecy + sovereignty between life and death  
- Native American = stealing light from darkness and giving it to everyone

Raven MCP: design intelligence locked in expert heads, now flying across your work and whispering what it knows.

## How the MCP Maps to the Mythology

### Huginn (Thought/Knowledge) = Principles & Patterns Layer
get_principles, get_pattern, evaluate_design — the raven that flies out and returns with knowledge of what works, what fails, and why. Pure observation across UX law, Gestalt, accessibility, conversion science.

### Muninn (Memory) = Tokens & Design Systems Layer
get_design_system, list_design_systems, compose_system — institutional memory of the world's finest design craft. Stripe's color scales, Linear's type precision, spacing systems refined over years. Odin fears losing Muninn more — you can re-derive a principle, but you cannot re-derive a mature design system from first principles.

### The Nine Realms = Knowledge Layers
- Principles = Asgard (highest order, laws of perception)
- Patterns = Midgard (human world, proven solutions)
- Business = Jötunheim (forces you negotiate with)
- Tokens = Niðavellir (dwarven forges where artifacts are made)
search_knowledge crosses all realms in a single flight.

### The Runes = Design Tokens
Odin hung on Yggdrasil 9 nights for the runes — compressed symbols of encoded power. Design tokens are runes: `--stripe-color-blue-500: #635bff` is brand strategy, perceptual testing, accessibility math, and competitive positioning collapsed into six characters. compose_system = runic inscription from multiple traditions.

### Hrafnaguð (Raven-God) = Claude + Raven MCP
The AI with ravens on its shoulders. Before Raven: wise but blind. After Raven: every design question gets the full flight across all realms.

### Hrafnsmerki (Raven Banner) = get_checklist
Raise the banner before shipping. If the raven flies (checklist passes), ship. If it hangs limp, don't.

### The Morrígan's Sovereignty = Democratized Craft
Design craft sovereignty was gatekept by access to senior designers and elite agencies. Raven democratizes it — the Morrígan's sovereignty flies to anyone who calls.

### Yéil Steals the Sun = The Origin Story
Design knowledge locked in bentwood boxes (agency playbooks, enterprise DS teams, senior designers' heads). Raven broke the boxes open. Ten tools. Three knowledge layers. Light for everyone.
