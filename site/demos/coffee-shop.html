Our Process

From farm to your cup

Every bag we roast follows the same three principles that have guided us since day one.

01

Source

We travel to origin, build relationships with farming families, and pay above fair-trade prices. Every bean is traceable to the lot it was grown on.

02

Roast

Micro-batches of 25 pounds or less, roasted on a vintage Probat. We profile each lot individually to find its sweetest expression.

03

Brew

We ship within 48 hours of roasting so you get peak flavor. Brewing guides included with every order to help you get the perfect cup at home.
